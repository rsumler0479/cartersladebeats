FAQs

Why is Carter Slade Beats a good option for my next project?


Carter Slade Beats believes in commiting 100% into producing quality beats that give your listeners sounds that compliment your style of musical creativity.

Who owns the beat ?


Carter Slade Beats offers three simple licensing options for our beat offerings. These three options include leasing the beat for temporary use , purchasing exclusive rights for a beat which removes the beat for further use from CarterSladeBeats.com , and the third option is using a beat with one of our sponsors in the tag (this option will be launched soon). All beats are ultimately owned by CarterSladeBeats.com and are open for complete ownership through negotion with site administrators.

Am I able to purchase a custom made beat ?


Yes. A Carter Slade Beats representative will assist you with the process of deciding a style of song you'd like to make , how to best format your voice to the beat's sound , & mastering the final result. All custom beat requests should be sent to site administrators at cartersladegroup@protonmail.com.
